With innovation at its core, ISRO is shaping the future of space travel, satellite communication, and interplanetary research.

India's first rocket, Nike-Apache, was so small and lightweight that its parts were transported on a bicycle to the launch pad at Thumba Equatorial Rocket Launching Station (TERLS) in Kerala. This historic launch took place on November 21, 1963, marking India's humble yet ambitious entry into space exploration.

I-Grasp

Indian Geo-Platform for Assessment, Monitoring, and Planning

A GIS-based platform for geospatial data analysis and decision-making.

Helps in planning and monitoring developmental projects.

Utilized in agriculture, urban planning, and disaster management.

ISRO STEM

Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics

A platform to encourage students in STEM education related to space science.

Provides online courses, webinars, and interactive learning materials.

Includes activities like quizzes, space-related projects, and competitions.

SPARK

Stark/ISRO's Space Technology Applications Repository

A knowledge-sharing portal that showcases space technology applications.

Helps industries, academia, and startups access ISRO’s research for practical applications.

Provides case studies and domain-specific solutions.

MOSDAC

Meteorological & Oceanographic Satellite Data Archival Center

Focuses on satellite data related to meteorology, oceanography, and environmental studies.

Provides real-time weather updates, cyclone tracking, and ocean parameters.

ISSDC

Indian Space Science Data Centre

The primary data archive for ISRO’s space science missions.

Stores and provides access to data from missions like Chandrayaan, Mangalyaan, and Astrosat.

Supports scientific research by making space mission data publicly available.

Bhoonidhi

ISRO’s Geospatial Data Marketplace

A portal to access and purchase ISRO’s satellite imagery and geospatial data.

Provides high-resolution images for applications like land monitoring, infrastructure development, and disaster management.

Used by government agencies, researchers, and businesses for GIS-based solutions.

Bhuvan

ISRO's geoportal providing satellite imagery, GIS tools, and spatial data.

Used for applications like disaster management, urban planning, and agriculture monitoring.

Offers features like 3D visualization, terrain mapping, and thematic services.
